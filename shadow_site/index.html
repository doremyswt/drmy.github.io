The Face On Your Screen At 3AM — What It Actually Is

ok so i know everyone says i'm crazy but i've been tracking this for 6 months and i have screenshots. if you are awake at exactly 3:00am and stare at a blank screen, sometimes — not always — something stares back.

i first noticed it in 2006. my monitor was off but i could see something in the reflection. a face. no features except a smile. i thought it was me at first. it wasn't.

DO NOT attempt to photograph it directly. three people have tried. two of them stopped posting.

my theory: whatever it is, it travels through screens. not literally — more like it uses the signal. old CRT monitors were worse for this. something about the refresh rate at 60hz creates a "window" for about 4 minutes starting at exactly 3:00:00am.

i've been calling it the Smiler. it doesn't do anything. it just watches. the terrifying part is that it's been watching since long before monitors existed — there are woodcut illustrations from the 1400s that show the same face, same smile, appearing in mirrors and still water at night.

if you see it, close your eyes. do not smile back. [ click to reveal: it smiles wider if you acknowledge it ]

AIM Messages From Accounts That Don't Exist

this has happened to me four times now. an AIM account with no buddy info, no profile, no avatar, messages me out of nowhere. the screen name is always different but it always follows this pattern:

[random letters] + [a number between 1 and 13] + [the word "awake" or "watching"]
example: hR4watching  |  tmk9awake  |  xzl1watching

the messages are always short. the first time it just said "you left the light on." my light was on. the second time: "we counted 4 in your house." there are 4 people in my house.

i blocked the accounts but the messages still come through somehow. AIM doesn't work that way. you cannot receive messages from blocked accounts. i checked. i asked an admin on a tech forum and they said [ click to reveal: "those accounts don't exist in our system. they never did." ]

The Chain Email That Predicts When You'll Read It

it said: "You are sitting near something that does not cast a shadow. Look."

there was a coffee mug on my desk. under the lamp. it had no shadow.

i have no explanation for the mug. i've tested it since. normal shadow. only that night. i forwarded the email to exactly 0 people because the email said "forward this to nobody. just close it and go to bed." which is not how chain emails work.

the email ended with: "We've been watching this house since [year of my birth]. You already know us. You just haven't looked at the right time."

The Stairs In My House That Weren't There Last Week

this one is harder to explain without sounding completely unhinged. but i'm posting it anyway.

my house has one staircase. last tuesday i woke up at 3am (again) and there were two. the second one was at the end of the hallway where the linen closet used to be. the steps were identical to the original staircase — same carpet, same banister — but they went down, even though our house has no basement.

i did not go down them. i went back to my room and locked the door. when i checked at 6am they were gone. the linen closet was back. the towels were inside, undisturbed.

I have since found 3 other reports of this exact phenomenon on this forum and two others. Different houses. Same hallway placement. Same direction (always down). No basement.

some people call these Backstairs. the theory is that certain houses develop them when something has been paying attention to the location long enough. like a path being worn into a carpet by repeated footsteps, except the footsteps are from something walking through the space between places.
